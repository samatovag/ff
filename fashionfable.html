Style Advice

  • Eco-friendly and sustainable fashion continues to be a major focus. Look for brands that prioritize ethical practices, recycled materials, and timeless pieces that won’t go out of style.
  • Tailoring is making a strong comeback. Structured blazers, high-waisted trousers, and well-fitted coats are great investments. They add polish and can be dressed up or down.
  • Layering. Master the art of layering to create dynamic outfits. Combine textures and lengths for a look that’s visually interesting and practical for changing weather.
  • Minimalist elegance. For those who prefer a more understated look, minimalist fashion is still very much in vogue. Clean lines, monochromatic outfits, and simple, well-made pieces are always in style.

  • The focus on comfort remains strong. Think stylish yet comfortable pieces like oversized sweaters, relaxed-fit trousers, and slip-on shoes. The goal is to look good while feeling great.
